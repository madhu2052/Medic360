E-Prescription

E-Prescription, is a centralized paperless system for issuing and handling medical prescriptions. When a doctor prescribes medicine using the system, he or she does so electronically, with the aid of an online form. At the pharmacy, all a patient needs to do is present an unique prescription number. The pharmacist then retrieves the patient’s information from the system and issues the medicine.
